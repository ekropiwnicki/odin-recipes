Lasagna Recipe

A delicious combination of cheese, tomatoes, and beef

Back in Italy, during Mussolini's time, grandmama would always make her delicious homemade lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups of tomatoes
  • 4 cups of shredded mozzarella
  • 1 pound ground beef

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 500F
  2. Chop tomatoes
  3. Throw tomatoes, shredded mozzarella and beef into a baking dish
  4. Cook for 30 minutes
